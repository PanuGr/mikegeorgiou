Our Story

Founded in 2015 by a creative team of seasoned game designers from Greece and Finland, what started as a small collective of friends sharing ideas around a kitchen table has evolved into a respected studio. We're passionate game enthusiasts with experience across diverse projects, dedicated to creating engaging and memorable gaming experiences. Our love for storytelling, strategic gameplay, and beautiful design has been at the forefront of our work, and we deliver high-quality board games refined through multiple playtests, with a focus on the player experience.

Over the past decade, we've created games that challenge minds, spark imagination, and, most importantly, bring people together. We are proud to offer custom-tailored collaborations, crafting unique games that match our clients’ exact needs. Our commitment to quality and innovation has earned us recognition in the gaming community and countless hours of joy shared around tables worldwide.

What We Do

From concept to completion, we handle every aspect of board game creation

  • Game Development: Complete game development from concept to finished product, handling every technical aspect.
  • Game Design:Creating engaging games from scratch with innovative mechanics and compelling gameplay.
  • Playtesting:Rigorous testing processes to ensure balanced, fun, and polished gameplay experiences.
  • Digital Implementations:Tabletop Simulator modding and digital versions of your board games.
  • Product Design:Professional product design ensuring your game looks great and functions perfectly.
  • Rulebook Editing:Clear, concise rulebook editing to make your games easy to learn and teach.
  • Graphic Design & Illustration:Professional graphic design and illustration coordination to bring your vision to life.
  • Crowdfunding Consulting:Expert guidance for successful crowdfunding campaigns from strategy to execution.
  • Manufacturing Consulting:Navigate the manufacturing process with our expertise in production and quality control.
  • Fulfillment Consulting:Streamlined fulfillment strategies to get your games to customers efficiently.

Meet Our Team

The creative minds behind BoardCraft Studios

Argyris (or Argy) Poungouras

A game designer and developer based in Athens, Greece. He worked on design and development on games such as Darkest Dungeon: The Board Game and Super Fantasy Brawl published by Mythic Games, Back O’ Beyond by Fabled Games and numerous other projects. He draws inspiration from all aspects of gaming, be it board games, video games, miniature games or card games. He loves creating original works as well as adapting existing IPs to the board game medium. Argy is very active in his local designer and playtest communities and always keeps himself updated on the state of the industry.
