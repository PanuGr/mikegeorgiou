Our Story

Founded in 2015 by a group of passionate game enthusiasts, BoardCraft Studios has been at the forefront of innovative board game design and development. What started as a small collective of friends sharing ideas around a kitchen table has evolved into a respected studio known for creating engaging, memorable gaming experiences.

Over the past decade, we've combined our love for storytelling, strategic gameplay, and beautiful design to create games that challenge minds, spark imagination, and most importantly, bring people together. Our commitment to quality and innovation has earned us recognition in the gaming community and countless hours of joy shared around tables worldwide.

Meet Our Team

Argyris (or Argy) Poungouras

job title

A game designer and developer based in Athens, Greece. He worked on design and development on games such as Darkest Dungeon: The Board Game and Super Fantasy Brawl published by Mythic Games, Back O’ Beyond by Fabled Games and numerous other projects. He draws inspiration from all aspects of gaming, be it board games, video games, miniature games or card games. He loves creating original works as well as adapting existing IPs to the board game medium. Argy is very active in his local designer and playtest communities and always keeps himself updated on the state of the industry.
